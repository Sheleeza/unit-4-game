Instructions

You will be given a random number to start the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number to your score. You win if your total score matches the goal number or you lose if your score is higher than the goal number. The value of the crystal is hidden from you before each game starts and it will change with each game.
